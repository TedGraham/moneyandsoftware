    Donate your old computer

    Ted Graham April 18, 2008

    I posted a craigslist ad yesterday to get rid of a couple of old desktop computers that have been sitting idle in my basement.  Despite the machines being obsolete and not having monitors, I got 42 emails overnight from people who wanted them.  The responses were all over the place, including:
    - the local homeless coalition
    - the mother of a disabled boy who wants a computer to write poems
    - the Girl Scouts
    - some students at a tech college who rebuild computers and donate them to non-profits.

    I'd bet most of you have an old computer you aren't using.  Thanks for considering donating.
